CHAPTER TEN: FIND THE BLACK SWAN

In this chapter, Chris Voss discusses the importance of finding the "black swan" in a negotiation, which refers to an unexpected alternative that meets the other party's needs.

Key lessons from this chapter summarized by ChatGPT:

  1. Finding the "black swan" can break deadlocks and create new possibilities in negotiation: By offering an unexpected alternative that meets the other party's needs, you can break deadlocks and create new possibilities in the negotiation.
  2. Use "black swans" to gather information: Asking open-ended questions and using "black swans" to explore the other party's needs and interests can help you gather more information and better understand their perspective.
  3. Be creative when finding "black swans": It's important to be creative and think outside the box when finding "black swans", as this can help you come up with unexpected alternatives that meet the other party's needs.
  4. Use "black swans" in combination with other negotiation techniques: "Black swans" can be most effective when used in combination with other negotiation techniques, such as mirroring and emotion labeling.
